Got questions?

How do I create a playlist?

Playlists are a great way to save collections of music
either for your own listening or to share.

To create one:

  1. Tap Your Library.
  2. Tap CREATE.
  3. Give your playlist a name.
  4. Start adding songs (and we'll help you along).
How do I activate Data Saver mode?
  1. Tap home
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Tap Data Saver.
  4. Switch on Data Saver.
Is it only possible to shuffle play music?

Any playlist with the shuffle icon will play on shuffle
Some playlists won't have the shuffle
icon so you can tap any song to play it.

Where can I find Podcasts?

Tap Search. Under Browse All, tap Podcasts.
